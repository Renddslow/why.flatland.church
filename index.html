What is Why?

Why? is a collection of 7 incredible stories from Flatland Church designed to reach your neighbors in the greater Omaha area.

Each story in Why? is a true story of someone from our Flatland family who has experienced the transformational power of Jesus Christ, and we want to share that with the whole city.

How Can I Get Involved?

Why? is 100% powered by you. With every $10 you donate, 1 book will be sent to a home in Omaha who may never have heard the Gospel before.

To participate in this project to the fullest, we recommend signing up to sponsor a number of books on a monthly basis.

Introduction

Why do bad things happen to good people?

Why do good people do bad things?

Why do some people get all the luck?

These questions, and many more like them, have perplexed human beings throughout the ages. Philosophers have offered their ideas. Theologians have debated their doctrines. Scientists have analyzed their hypotheses. But still, the questions remain.

If you wrestle with questions like these, then this book is for you. The following pages reveal true stories from some of your neighbors right here in Omaha. As you read about their tragic circumstances, horrific experiences, and miraculous triumphs, the whys of life will be stirred up and brought to the surface. And perhaps, in the pages of this book, you'll find answers to life's most perplexing questions.
